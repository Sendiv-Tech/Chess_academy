Our Story

Where Passion Meets Precision

"Chess is life in miniature. Chess is a struggle, chess battles."

Founded in 2006 by International Master Rajesh Venkatesh, Chess Academy has grown from a small training group into Tamil Nadu's most respected chess institution. We combine classical techniques with modern computational analysis to build players who think sharper, faster, and more creatively.

Our alumni include state champions, national-level competitors, and FIDE-rated players who continue to represent India on global stages.
