What We Offer

World-Class Facilities

Every element of our campus is designed to create the optimal environment for learning and competition.

🏛️

Main Training Hall

A climate-controlled, distraction-free hall with 40 professional-grade DGT chess sets, demo boards, and padded seating for extended sessions.

💻

Digital Analysis Lab

20 workstations powered by Stockfish 17 engine, ChessBase, and Lichess integrations for deep game analysis and opening preparation.

🏆

Tournament Arena

An FIDE-compliant 80-board tournament area with wall clocks, demonstration boards, and live result display — ideal for internal championships.

📚

Chess Library

Over 600 titles from classic Silman and Nimzowitsch to contemporary Gawain Jones series. Available to all enrolled students.

🎥

Live Streaming Studio

Our online classes are broadcast from a professional studio with multi-camera setup, ensuring crystal-clear instruction for remote students.

☕

Student Lounge

A comfortable rest area for students between sessions, equipped with casual blitz boards and a small snack station. Chess culture lives here too.
