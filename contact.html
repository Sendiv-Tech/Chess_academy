FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to what prospective students and parents ask most.

What is the minimum age for enrollment?
We accept students from age 6 onwards. Our youngest learners join our Weekend Junior Batch, which uses games and puzzles to make learning fun and age-appropriate.
Do I need any prior chess knowledge?
Not at all. Our Beginner program starts from scratch — we cover piece movements, rules, basic strategies, and your first games. Complete beginners are very welcome.
Are online classes as effective as offline?
For theory, analysis, and tactics training, online classes are equally effective. For young children under 10, we recommend at least some offline sessions for better engagement and hands-on board experience.
What are the fees?
Fees vary by program level, batch size, and format (online vs offline). Group classes start from ₹2,500/month. Contact us or attend a free demo session and we'll share a detailed fee structure tailored to your needs.
How does the free demo session work?
The demo is a 45-minute session where you (or your child) joins a regular class at no cost. Our coach will assess the student's current level and recommend the ideal batch. No commitment required after the demo.
Do you provide tournament participation support?
Yes. We guide students through AICF registration, help select suitable tournaments, and provide specific preparation coaching before each event. Tournament fees are separate from coaching fees.
